When Your Sample Arrives
  • Refrigerate immediately. Store at 36–40 °F in the clamshell or a damp towel.
  • Use within 7–10 days for peak flavor and texture.
  • Add at the finish. Micro cilantro is best as a raw garnish — place it after plating, not during cooking.
  • Taste it straight. Try a few leaves on their own so you can calibrate how much to use per plate.
